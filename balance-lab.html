Developer Tools

Balance Lab

Ready to stage hidden balance trials.

Unit Pressure

Bias compares a unit's win rate to the sampled field average. Qty Corr shows whether a unit tends to improve when you bring more of it (`+`) or keep it sparse (`-`).

Army Compositions

This ranks the most reliable full compositions seen so far, using their running battle results across all samples.

Pair Synergies

Two-unit pairings are compared against their members' average solo win tendency. Green pairs overperform together; red pairs drag each other down.

Counter Matrix

Shows when armies containing one unit consistently beat armies featuring another unit in the same sampled heats.

Unit Winrate Trends

Tracks each unit's running win rate as more battles complete, making it easier to see whether results are still wandering or settling into a stable lane.
