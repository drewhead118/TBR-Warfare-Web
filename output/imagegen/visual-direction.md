# TBR Warfare Visual Direction

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: game art bible and production prompts
Primary request: premium 2.5D top-down fantasy war simulation used to select the next book in a TBR queue
Scene/background: painterly battlefield clearing seen from a high 2.5D angle with soft hills, trampled grass, warm banners, drifting dust, and magical impact glows
Subject: small armies representing books, with readable silhouettes for archers, mages, and knights; each army carries a banner featuring the book cover art
Style/medium: hand-painted stylized 3D concept art with readable game silhouettes, premium board-game-meets-animated-film art direction
Composition/framing: top-down battlefield shots, isolated unit sheets, UI illustrations, effect sheets, and banner mockups; strong readability at small scale
Lighting/mood: late-afternoon golden light with crisp highlights, soft long shadows, and restrained magical bloom
Color palette: parchment, warm gold, moss green, weathered bronze, desaturated teal magic, ember orange archery accents
Materials/textures: painted cloth banners, worn leather, brushed steel, glowing crystal cores, dusty ground, parchment UI
Quality: high
Constraints: cohesive across gameplay and UI, small-scale readability first, premium but charming rather than gritty, designed for 2.5D perspective
Avoid: muddy silhouettes, noisy details, generic mobile-fantasy excess, purple-heavy palettes, overly dark scenes, grim realism
